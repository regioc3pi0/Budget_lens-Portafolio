# ✨ Features — Budget Lens

---

## 🔍 Capture Tools

### Assisted Scanner (OCR)

Point your camera at any receipt and the app automatically extracts the total
and date. You just type the concept, select the category and account, confirm,
and done. Works best with clear, well-lit receipts.

### Voice Command Dictation

Log expenses and income by speaking naturally — no need to touch the screen.
Dictate in this order: Action, Amount, Concept, Category, Account, and Date.

Examples:
- "Expense, 180, Tacos, Food, Cash, Today"
- "Income, 5000, Bonus, Salary, Payroll, December 22 2025"

### Manual Entry

Your flexible option for when scanning isn't precise, or when you need to log
an expense without a receipt — like tips or cash payments.

---

## 📊 Financial Control Dashboard

### Real Balance vs. Cash Flow

Clearly distinguish between your accumulated net worth and what you've moved
this month. Don't confuse account balance with financial health.

### Configurable Reminders

Set manual alerts for your important recurring services — internet, rent,
streaming — and avoid late fees from forgotten due dates.

---

## 🎯 Visual Budgets

Set spending limits for categories like "Food" or "Entertainment".
A progress bar shows you in real time how much is left before you hit
your limit.

Stop blowing your paycheck in a single weekend.

---

## 📅 Payment Projection

Track your fixed expenses — streaming, utilities, rent — and see exactly
how much you need to set aside over the next 15 days to cover all your
upcoming bills without surprises.

---

## 🔒 100% Private & Secure (Offline)

Your data lives only on your iPhone. No external servers are used,
nothing is uploaded to the cloud without your consent.

- Face ID and Touch ID support
- Local backups included
- No access to banking data
