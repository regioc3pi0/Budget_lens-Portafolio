Tired of saving receipts and writing down every expense at the end of the day?
Budget Lens removes the friction from personal finance using your iPhone's
built-in technology. Unlike other apps that just add up numbers, we give you a
real picture of your finances — from how much money you have available today,
to visual alerts before you go over budget.

You choose how to log — scan a receipt, dictate with your voice, or enter
manually. No external servers. No friction. Just control.
